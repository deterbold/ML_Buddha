This app is an obscure, unfunny joke played on Nam June Paik's classic work TV Buddha.

Instructions:

  1. Start the camera by tapping on the "Search for Buddha" button, and point it to a Buddha figure.
  2. Once the Machine Learning model has identified a Buddha figure, it will give you 10 seconds to turn the phone so you can use the front camera.
  3. Point the front camera to the Buddha statue. Sounds will play. Keep it there.
  4. It could be funny.
  5. If the Machine Learning model stops recognizing a Buddha, the app will return to searching mode.
